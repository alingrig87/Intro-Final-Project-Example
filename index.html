About Romania

Romania is a country located in Southeastern Europe, known for its rich history, diverse landscapes, medieval castles, and charming cities. From the Carpathian Mountains to the Black Sea, Romania has something for every traveler.

Popular Cities

  • Bucharest

    The capital city of Romania, known for its impressive Parliament Palace, vibrant nightlife, and historical buildings.

  • Cluj-Napoca

    Heart of Transylvania, famous for its universities, cultural life, and student atmosphere.

  • Brasov

    Located near the Carpathians, Brasov is a medieval city with cobblestone streets, charming squares, and the Black Church.

Interesting Facts

  • Romania is home to the largest building in Europe - the Palace of the Parliament.
  • It has one of the best-preserved medieval towns in Europe: Sighișoara.
  • Transylvania is famous for the Dracula legend and Bran Castle.
  • Romania has over 13 national parks and many natural reserves.
